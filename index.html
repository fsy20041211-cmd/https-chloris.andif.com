CHLORIS

Eau de Parfum
Inspired by new green and summer air

On a quiet summer afternoon,
warm wind passes through citrus trees
and old stone houses. Inspired by Chloris, the nymph of new green in Greek mythology, this fragrance captures the moment when spring slowly becomes summer.

Fresh lemon and bergamot recall the sunlight of southern Italy. Petitgrain and juniper evoke olive leaves and cool shadows. Soft florals and woods linger like skin warmed by the Mediterranean sun.

Chloris is not a place, but the feeling of a summer that stays with you.

Warm air, citrus trees,
and the quiet rhythm of summer.

Inspired by Chloris and the slow, sun-drenched summers of the Mediterranean.
