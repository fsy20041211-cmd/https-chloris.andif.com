CHLORIS

On a quiet summer afternoon,
warm wind passes through citrus trees
and old stone houses.

Fresh lemon and bergamot recall sunlight.
Green leaves, soft woods, and skin-warm air.

Chloris is not a place,
but the feeling of a summer that stays.
